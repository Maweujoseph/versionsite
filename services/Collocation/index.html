Collocation and Cloud Services

Colocation facilities provide space, power, cooling, and physical security for the server, storage, and networking equipment of other firms and also connect them to a variety of telecommunications and network service providers with a minimum of cost and complexity.

We have established fully equipped Data Centers in Kenya located in Westlands(Royal Offices), Airport North Road and Mombasa(Pwani Data Center), providing Collocation, Cloud and Managed Services. Customer’s equipment and Data are hosted in environmentally controlled space with the following features:

  • Air conditioning
  • Fire detection and suppression
  • AC/DC power
  • Controlled access/security systems
  • Shared rack space or dedicated racks/suites to suit customer requirements
  • Simplified interconnection with Frontier Optical’s Network
  • Skilled engineers providing 24-hour technical support and Remote Hands & Eyes
